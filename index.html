About Dalph

We believe that technology is a powerful force for good and we are working to foster a sustainable and level playing platform where everyone have access to and can be empowered by the benefits and opportunities created by technology. we are leveraging on technology to provide a number one go to market platform where talents, skills, services and expertise meet demands, thereby creating economic opportunities and lifestyle.

Our Vision

To be the number one market place for in-demand home services, where everyone can shop for home services on the go without breaking a sweat.

Our Mission

To empower everyone to work their passion and to continously deliver value to our client's experience.

What We Do

At Dalph, we help every home to get more done by providing reliable access to a large pool of seasoned talents, skills, services and professionalism and; giving those offering in-demand home services the freedom to look for and find jobs online with ease. This we do by connecting proven talents, skills, and services to homes demanding them.

Our Core Values

Our core values shape our organization's culture and define the character of our company. We live the core vlaues through individual behaviour and they serve as the basis for how we act and make decisions.

Client Obsession
Our clients are our most prized assets. We are determined to create meaningful relationships with our clients by being responsive, staying relevant and by continously delivering value to our client's experience.
Respect
We understand that the opinions, feelings, and circumstances of others are as important as ours and this we do by valuing diversity and unque contributions, finding common grounds, nurturing a trusting, open and inclusive environment and treating each person in a manner that reflects Dalph's values.
Intergrity
We conduct ourselves honestly in all things, and we let our actions speak for who we are and what we believe in. We are uncompromising in our ideals and we inspire trust by being consistent with our core values, matching our behaviours with our words and accepting responsibility for our actions.
Accountability and Stewardship
We believe accountability begins with us and we accept full responsibilty for our decisions, actions, and results. We also feel strongly obliged to fulfill our company's vision of building a one stop market place where every person and every home can buy or sell in-demand home talents, skills and services, protecting our customers' interests and experiences and meeting our commitment to bringing economic opportunity and lifestyle to everyone.
